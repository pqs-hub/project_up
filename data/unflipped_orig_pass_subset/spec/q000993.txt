Design a combinational logic circuit that implements a simple Dynamic Voltage and Frequency Scaling (DVFS) controller. The circuit takes an input voltage level (3-bit) and output a scaled frequency level (2-bit) based on the following logic:  
- If the voltage is 0 (000), frequency is 0 (00)  
- If the voltage is 1 (001), frequency is 1 (01)  
- If the voltage is 2 (010), frequency is 2 (10)  
- If the voltage is 3 (011) or 4 (100), frequency is 3 (11)  
- If the voltage is 5 (101) or 6 (110), frequency is limited to 3 (11)  
- If the voltage is 7 (111), frequency is limited to 0 (00)
