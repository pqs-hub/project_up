Design a fan speed controller that adjusts the speed of a fan based on a temperature input. The controller should have three fan speed outputs: low, medium, and high. When the temperature is below 20 degrees Celsius, the fan should operate at low speed. Between 20 and 30 degrees, it should operate at medium speed. Above 30 degrees, the fan should operate at high speed. Use a clock signal to ensure that the fan speed changes only on the rising edge of the clock.
