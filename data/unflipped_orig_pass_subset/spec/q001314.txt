Design a 16-bit DRAM cell that can perform read and write operations. The DRAM cell should have the following inputs: `clk` (clock), `we` (write enable), `addr` (address, 4 bits), and `data_in` (data input, 16 bits). It should output `data_out` (data output, 16 bits) which is the stored data at the address when `we` is low (read operation). When `we` is high (write operation), the `data_in` should be stored at the given `addr`.
