Design an 8-bit Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) that converts an input voltage level (given as an 8-bit binary number) to a corresponding digital output in a specific format. The ADC should take an 8-bit binary number as input and produce a 4-bit output representing the upper half of the input number (the most significant 4 bits) and a 4-bit output representing the lower half (the least significant 4 bits). The output should be valid only when an enable signal is high.
