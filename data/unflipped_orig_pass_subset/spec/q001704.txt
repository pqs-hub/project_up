Design a digital phase shifter that takes a 3-bit input representing a phase (0 to 7) and outputs a 3-bit phase-shifted value based on a given control signal. The control signal will determine the direction of the phase shift: if the control signal is 0, the phase will be shifted right (increasing the phase), and if the control signal is 1, the phase will be shifted left (decreasing the phase). The phase shift will be by 1 step (i.e., +1 or -1). Make sure to wrap around the phase values (0 to 7).
