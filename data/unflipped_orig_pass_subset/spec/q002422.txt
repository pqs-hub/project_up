Design a simple 8-bit Analog-to-Digital Converter (ADC) simulator. The ADC will take a 3-bit input representing the voltage level and output an 8-bit digital representation. The mapping will be as follows:  
- 000 -> 00000000  
- 001 -> 00111111  
- 010 -> 01111111  
- 011 -> 01111111  
- 100 -> 11111111  
Any input above 4 will output 11111111. The output must be left-aligned, meaning the least significant bits will be zeroes.
