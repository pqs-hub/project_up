Design a Low Dropout Regulator (LDO) that takes an input voltage (Vin), a reference voltage (Vref), and an enable signal (EN). The LDO should output the regulated voltage (Vout) which will be equal to Vref when EN is high and Vin is greater than Vref, otherwise Vout should be 0. If Vin is less than Vref and EN is high, Vout should also be 0.
