Design a Verilog module that performs a 2x2 LU decomposition on a given matrix. The input to the module will be a 2x2 matrix represented by four inputs (a, b, c, d) and the output will be two matrices L (lower triangular) and U (upper triangular) represented as:
- L = | l11  0  |
-     | l21  l22 |
  
- U = | u11  u12 |
-     | 0    u22 |

Where l11 = 1, l21 = c/a, l22 = d - (c*b)/a, u11 = a, u12 = b, and u22 = d - (c*b)/a. You may assume that 'a' is non-zero for the division to be valid.
