Design an Up/Down counter in Verilog that counts up when a control signal (`up`) is high and counts down when it is low. The counter should reset to zero when a reset signal (`reset`) is high. The counter should be 4 bits wide and should have a maximum count of 15. If the counter exceeds 15 while counting up, it should wrap around to 0. If it goes below 0 while counting down, it should also wrap around to 15.
