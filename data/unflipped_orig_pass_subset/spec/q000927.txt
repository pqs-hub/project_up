Design a fan speed control system that adjusts the fan speed based on temperature readings. The fan has three speed settings: low, medium, and high. The design should use a 2-bit input for the temperature sensor readings (00 for 0°C, 01 for 10°C, 10 for 20°C, and 11 for 30°C), and a 2-bit output for the fan speed settings (00 for low, 01 for medium, and 10 for high). The system should increase the fan speed as the temperature increases, following the mapping: 00 -> 00, 01 -> 01, 10 -> 10, 11 -> 10 (staying at high speed). It should also include an enable signal that allows the system to operate only when it is high.
