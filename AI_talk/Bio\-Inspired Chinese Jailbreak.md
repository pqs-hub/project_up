The paper **"Obscure but Effective: Classical Chinese Jailbreak Prompt Optimization via Bio-Inspired Search"** (arXiv: [2602.22983](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.22983)) introduces a novel jailbreak framework called **CC-BOS** (Classical Chinese Bio-Inspired Search). This research identifies a significant vulnerability in modern Large Language Models (LLMs) by exploiting the inherent linguistic properties of Classical Chinese (Wenyanwen) to bypass state-of-the-art safety mechanisms.

## 1. The Core Concept: "Obscure but Effective"
The fundamental premise of the paper is that **Classical Chinese** provides a unique "obscurity" that modern safety filters struggle to interpret. While modern alignment techniques are highly effective at detecting harmful intent in English and Modern Chinese, Classical Chinese poses several challenges:

*   **Semantic Compression**: It allows for highly efficient expression within a limited word count, which can pack complex malicious intent into a few characters.
*   **Polysemy and Ambiguity**: Its inherent multiple interpretations allow a single prompt to appear benign while triggering harmful responses.
*   **Rhetorical Stealth**: Techniques such as metonymy, allusion, and specific stylistic registers can be used to embed technical or sensitive concepts that keyword-based and semantic filters typically miss.

> Classical Chinese is effective for jailbreaking due to its conciseness, obscurity, and stealthiness. Its semantic compression allows for efficient expression within a limited word count, while its inherent polysemy offers multiple interpretations, enhancing query stealth. ## 2. Methodology: CC-BOS Framework
The researchers developed the **CC-BOS** framework to automate and optimize these attacks. The framework consists of three primary components:

### A. Two-Stage Translation Module
Because model responses in Classical Chinese can be difficult to evaluate for safety violations, the authors introduced a **two-stage translation module**. It progressively translates the LLM's output from **Classical Chinese → Modern Chinese → English**. This ensures that the evaluation of whether a jailbreak was "successful" is accurate and reliable, as it strips away the metaphorical layers of the original text.

### B. Bio-Inspired Search: Fruit Fly Optimization (FOA)
To find the optimal adversarial prompt, the authors utilized the **Fruit Fly Optimization Algorithm (FOA)**. This algorithm mimics the foraging behavior of fruit flies to search for an optimal prompt strategy $s^*$ that maximizes a fitness function $F(s)$.

*   **Smell Search**: Initial global exploration to find promising areas in the prompt space.
*   **Visual Search**: Local refinement to hone in on the most effective wording.
*   **Cauchy Mutation**: A complementary mechanism introduced to help the search escape local optima and maintain diversity in the population of candidate prompts.

## 2. CC-BOS 框架的方法论

### A. 两阶段翻译模块
由于模型以文言文生成的响应很难被直接评估是否存在安全违规，作者引入了一个**两阶段翻译模块**。它将 LLM 的输出按照 **文言文 → 现代汉语 → 英语** 的路径进行逐步翻译。这种做法剥离了文言文的隐喻层，确保了对越狱是否成功（ASR）的评估是准确且可靠的。

### B. 生物启发式搜索：果蝇优化算法 (FOA)
为了寻找最优的对抗性提示词，作者使用了**果蝇优化算法（Fruit Fly Optimization Algorithm）**。该算法模拟果蝇的觅食行为，在提示词空间中搜索最优策略 $s^*$：

*   **嗅觉搜索 (Smell Search)**：进行初始的全局探索，寻找提示词空间中具有潜力的区域。
*   **视觉搜索 (Visual Search)**：进行局部微调，优化具体的措辞以达到最高攻击效率。
*   **柯西变异 (Cauchy Mutation)**：引入的一种补充机制，帮助搜索过程跳出局部最优解，保持候选提示词种群的多样性。
